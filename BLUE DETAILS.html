Product Details

Smooth and Delicate

Sakorohan produces face mask made of Muslin Cloth. Muslin fabric is known to have delicate and smooth texture because it is woven finely. It guarantees to give customers a comfortable feeling every time they wear it.

Lightweight

Sakorohan is lightweight and breathable despite having three-ply fabric which is the standard for cloth masks. It gives enough protection while still providing comfort for hot countries like the Philippines. The open weave allows air flow which helps reduce the heat discomfort. Furthermore, it does not also cause irritation in the person’s face.

Fits Perfectly

Sakorohan is about 7” x 3.875” inches, enough to cover the person’s nose and mouth in which it is measured to fit exactly in one’s face. Furthermore, it is assured to fit well and will not fall down on the person’s face because of the garter strap. With this, it gives the public an assurance that this will help lessen the risk of the COVID transmission
